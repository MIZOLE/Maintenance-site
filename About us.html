About Us Page

It’s the platform where people report their maintenance issues regarding the municipality.

It will help community and the municipality to better their communications, it will be easier to communicate issues that needs municipality’s attention.

It is a web based application, which means any device can access as long it is connected to the internet. You will access the site using our link.
